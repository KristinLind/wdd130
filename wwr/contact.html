Why Choose Us?

Our expert guides have been navigating the rivers of Utah since 2004. We offer guided rafting trips on Utah's most exciting and scenic rivers. Safe, fun, and accommodates all experience levels.Some of the most breathtaking scenery are only found while riding down the river. If you want thrills, we can accomodate.

If you would like someting that is a bit more calm, we can suggest the best trip for you. If you are one of those that enjoys a blend of short bursts of rapids followed by medium and calm waters, we offer trips for you as well. Our trips are some of the most unique and incredible experiences that you can find.
